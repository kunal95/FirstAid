Most nosebleeds are not serious and can be handled by a first aid responder. Most will stop on their own or with simple first aid actions. In some cases nosebleeds can indicate a more serious condition which may require ongoing medical attention.
First Aid Interventions
**Use the following interventions only if victim is alert – if not alert or appears confused, seek medical attention.
1. Five to six pieces of hard candy.
2. Four ounces of fruit juice or regular — not diet — soda.
3. One tablespoon of sugar, jelly or honey.
4. Three glucose tablets (available without a prescription at most pharmacies)
