Burns to the following areas (and sizes) must always be seen by a medical professional:
  • Head
  • Eyes
  • Foot
  • Groin
  • Neck
  • Hands
  • Joints
  • Burns over large area
Minor Burns
1. Expose the burn.
2. Cool burns with cold water and continue until pain lessens.
3. After cooling, cover with a dry, sterile bandage or clean dressing.
4. Protect from friction /pressure
5. DO NOT pop blisters or apply any ointment or other substance.
Major Burns
1. Assess/Alert/Attend to any life threatening problems.
2. Call emergency helpline.
3. If caused by a liquid chemical, flush with large amounts of water right away if you are fully trained in First Aid.
