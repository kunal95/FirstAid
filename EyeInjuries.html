Eye injuries can range from minor irritations to severe and sight threatening. Injuries are frequently caused by objects in the eye, burns, and blunt force injuries. Any of these conditions or situations can lead to permanent loss of vision.
First Aid Interventions
1. Rinse eye with saline solution or tap water if saline is not available.
2. Do not try to remove object.
3. Do not allow the victim to rub or apply pressure to the injured eye.
4. Cover the eye lightly with a gauze pad or clean cloth.
5. Seek medical attention.
