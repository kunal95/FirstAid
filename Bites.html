Bites and stings that could require first aid care can occur from a wide variety of sources. Most cause only minor discomfort and can easily be treated by a first aid provider. However bites and stings from venomous snakes, insects or animals can cause intense pain and swelling. Bites from humans and animals such as dogs, cats, bats, etc., can cause severe injury and infection, including tetanus and rabies.
General First Aid
1. Remove jewelry and constrictive clothing .
2. Wash the area with soap and clean water.
3. Cover the area with an adhesive bandage or gauze wrap.
4. Apply ice if needed to reduce pain and swelling.
